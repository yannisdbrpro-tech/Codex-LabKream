# KREAM Theme — Content Guide

## Collections
Use these exact collection handles when possible:
- ice-hustle-everyday
- signature-editions
- angel-hustle
- royal-drip
- tesser-lightning
- headwear
- outerwear
- essentials

## Products
Associate SKUs with their narratives:
- Crown Legacy Dad Hat — place in `headwear`.
- Doudoune Ice Hustle — place in `outerwear` and `ice-hustle-everyday`.
- Doudoune Street-Luxe “KREAM – Angel Hustle Edition” — place in `angel-hustle` and `outerwear`.
- Doudoune Street-Luxe “KREAM – Royal Drip” Edition Signature — place in `royal-drip`, `signature-editions`, and `outerwear`.
- Doudoune Street-Luxe “KREAM – Tesser-Lightning Edition Signature” — place in `tesser-lightning`, `signature-editions`, and `outerwear`.
- Sweatshirt “KREAM” – Signature Urbaine — place in `essentials` and `ice-hustle-everyday`.

## Copy Pillars
- Authority first: short, declarative lines.
- Emphasize Presence, Cut, Finish, Weight, Rarity on product cards and PDPs.
- Never use emojis or hype adjectives. Keep tone calm and commanding.

## Imagery
- Prefer high contrast visuals with black backgrounds and metallic highlights.
- Use alt text that states the garment, color, and collection (e.g., “Doudoune Ice Hustle in obsidian black”).
